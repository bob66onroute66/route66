On The Road To Memphis

From the parking lot at the Port Of L.A., we headed for Interstate 40. We had a good breakfast at Denny’s in Hesperia, Ca., just off of Interstate15.

From there we just drove straight through to Kingman, AZ on Interstate40. We had dinner at Floyd’s BBQ, located in downtown Kingman. Excellent BBQ. Highly recommended. We settled in for the night at the Red Roof Inn.
