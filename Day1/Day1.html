D ay 1; A Great Start

The first day was great. Saw a lot of fun things(listed above) and had a great lunch at Motorheads Bar and Grill in Springfield, Il. Highly recommended. Bought a cool Motorheads drink coaster for my friend, Leonard(he collects ‘em).

We ended up at the Wagon Wheel Motel in Cuba, Mo., which is right next to MO Hicks Bar-B-Q Restaurant. The Wagon Wheel is a quaint, cottage style buildings motel, taking you back to 50’s,60’s type motel comfort. It fit in well with our Route66 retro experience. We got a room, and literally walked over to the restaurant for dinner.

The food, service, everything was fantastic at MO Hicks. Highly recommended! We walked back to our room and settled in for a good nights sleep.
