Clovis, NM to Henryetta, OK

Mazzio's Pizza in Henryetta

Days Inn In Henryetta




Just Driving, Eating And Sleeping

We drove straight through to Henryetta, Oklahoma. We checked into a nice Days Inn (another one of the cheaper places to stay that was wonderful), and had a wonderful dinner at Mazzio’s Pizza.

Mazzio’s reminds me of our Pizza Huts in the Chicagoland area, some time ago. They have good pizza, and a nice salad bar. From their Website it shows a lot of locations in the middle of the US. I only found one location at the very southern tip of Illinois. Highland, Illinois.
