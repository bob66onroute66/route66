Start The Day!

We got up bright and early and began getting ready for the next day’s adventure. Dorreen hopped in the bathroom for a shower, while I got ready to brush my teeth and shave, using the sink just at the foot of the bed. I turned the water on, turning the hot faucet up all the way to gauge the water temperature. I heard the Shower water running in the bathroom, so everythings A-OK. I kept trying to get some hot water, but to no avail. I figured “well, maybe the hot water doesn’t work at this sink. I’ll just brush my teeth and shave with cold water, then when Dorreen’s done, take a nice warm shower.”

Dorreen barged out of the bathroom, “ There’s no hot water!”

I laughed, “wecome to life on the road.”

I was glad I hadn’t started shaving and we decided to skip the showers today and make sure our next sleepover stop had some warm water. The Wagon Wheel was a beautiful place to stay over night and I’m sure it was just a fluke that the water heater wasn’t working for that room. We recommend it; just make sure you check the water when you check in.

We hopped in the car and looked forward to another exciting day of wonder and adventure. We learned first hand that life on the road wouldn’t always been pretty.

Highlights

If you’re a fan of the old west, like I am, the Wild Bill Shootout Site is worth a stop. This is where Wild Bill Hickok had a quick draw duel over a watch. Wild Bill won.

The Worlds Biggest Gift Ship IS Huge, with lots of cool stuff. It’s kind of organized in themes, and has a lot of interesting items to check out. Worth a stop.

For lunch we love MO Hicks Barbeque!

We ended our day at a Microtel in Miami, Oklahoma. They have hot water.
